About Us

We're so much more than just cupcakes! Cupcake Cafe is a vegan bakery that sells a variety of desserts. We sell cupcakes all the way to macaroons!. Everything is homemade and vegan. We believe you can still enjoy desserts not at the expense of animals or the environment.
